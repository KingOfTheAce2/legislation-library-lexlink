Source: nl-nl_civil-procedure-code-2025 | Book: BOOK-1

Dutch (nl-nl)
Indien echter de waarborg geen verhaal biedt, moet de gewaarborgde de kosten dragen en, zo daartoe gronden zijn, ook de schade vergoeden.
English (en-gb)
However, where there are no assets against which recovery can be made against the indemnifying party, the indemnified party must bear the costs and, if warranted, pay damages.
